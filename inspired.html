Check out these role models
They used to be just like you!

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez is a U.S. representative for the 14th district of New York state. Cortez is a member of the democratic party and has expressed her advocacy for a change in policies regarding the economy, health care, and climate resolution.

Cameron Kasky is a gun control advocate, and survivor of the Parkland school shooting. Kasky, along with friends, started Never Again MSD which is a student-run organization to speak out against the lack of gun regulation.

Karlie Kloss is known for her work in the fashion and modeling industries but has been a supporting force in getting more women involved in STEM. Kode with Klossy was launched in 2015 as a way to empower women interested in computer science. The free two-week program gives girls an opportunity to learn how to code.

Mark Zuckerburg founded facebook. The media platform now has over 2 billion users. Zuckerburg launched the company after dropping out of college. Zuckerburg is also known for his work in philanthropy.

Greta Thunberg is a 16-year-old Swedish climate change activist. At age 15 she began speaking out and protesting for a response to the climate crisis. Thunberg inspired many to students and adults to protest for changes to be made.
